What if the inspection report reveals problems?


It is important to note that no house is perfect. Every home inspection will identify issues with the property and I will communicate the severity of the issues found. My goal is to leave you with a deeper understanding of your prospective home, so you can make a sound decision if you want to continue in the home buying process. You should be fully aware of any issues, risks, or health concerns that may impact the your decision. If major problems are found, you'll be able to negotiate with the seller to make repairs or cover your costs.
